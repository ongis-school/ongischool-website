    Tour Itinerary :

    DAY 01: BALI - ARRIVAL

    On arrival at Ngurah Rai Airport of Denpasar, our guide will meet you and transfer to your hotel.

    DAY 02: FULLDAY KINTAMANI VULCANO & LAKE TOUR

    Breakfast at hotel. Drive to Batubulan village to attend Barong and Keris dance performance. Then visit the art and handicraft villages of Celuk, Batuan, Mas and Ubud, to witness gold and silver work, hand weaving woodcarving and painting. Proceed to Goa Gajah (Elephant Cave), a Hindu meditation center dating back to the 11th Century, then Tampak Siring to see the holy spring water temple Tirta Empul. Next on to view the magnificent panorama of the still active Mt. Batur volcano and Lake Batur. Lunch at local restaurant. Drive through Bangli town to see the 13th century Kehen Temple. Return to hotel.

    DAY 03: RIVER RAFTING

    Breakfast at hotel. Enjoy exciting rafting with professional and experienced guides on Ayung River. Drive for 30 minutes to the North to the starting point (nearby Ubud ) then walk down through terraced path to the river. After introductory talk then enjoy exciting rafting for two hours until reach Kedewatan where we stop for having shower and enjoy buffet lunch at riverside restaurant. Return to hotel.

    DAY 04: ISLAND DAY CRUISE & LUNCH

    Breakfast at hotel. Cruise to Nusa Penida Island by a luxury catamaran ship to enjoy spectacular coral garden and colorful marine lives and other marine activities.

    DAY 05: BALI

    Breakfast at hotel. Full day free at leisure or join an optional tour to get to know more about Bali.

    DAY 06: BALI - OUT

    Breakfast at hotel. Transfer to the airport for your flight to next destination.

    Bali Fun Trips 5 Days

    Day 1: Ubud:

    Starting our first day by visiting the Ubud village as our first night staying. We are going to visit the Ubud local villages to enjoy its natural beauty as well as gaining some understanding on how the Balinese people lead their daily lives. Here we are going to visit UBUD MONKEY FORREST, a chance to feed the monkey at their home. If you are interesting we can add our program to visit the art villages close by such as CELUK for its gold and silver work, BATUBULAN for its famous stone sculptors, MAS well known as the central for the wood carving and furniture and UBUD itself for the home of the well known painters. Continuing our day tour we visit GUNUNG KAWI temple the 11th-century hermitage-imperial monument complex of AIRLANGGA dynasty. KINTAMANI to enjoying its spectacular panorama over looking the active volcano of mount BATUR. In the afternoon we are going to visit the PETULU village near Ubud to see thousands of WHITE HERON BIRD. Seeing the Balinese dance will be the highlight of our day. Overnight and dinner: UBUD.

    Day 2: Ubud/Amed

    After breakfast start the day east by visiting the ELEPHANT CAVE the 11th century Buddhist hermitage and monasteries. We drive on through the scenic villages of TEMBUKU enjoying its spectacular panorama overlooking the ocean, then we on to the slope of mount Agung to visit the mother temple of BESAKIH. Continuing our trip to AMED BEACH we are going to pass the beautiful villages of SELAT for its beautiful rice terrace view, PUTUNG a breathtaking panorama over looking the ocean, SIBETAN well known for its SALAK/SNAKE FRUIT cultivation, TIRTAGANGGA the water garden folly built by the last king of Karangasem " Gusti Jelantik " in the 19th century-often referred to as the Paris of Bali. Overnight and dinner: AMED.

    Day 3: Amed/Lovina

    Morning spends on the beach for snorkeling to enjoying the underwater world of Bali with its wonderful coral and fish. Spectacular drive along the coast lead us to the northern coast of Bali LOVINA a less touristy beach in Bali. Enroute we are going to make a stop at TULAMBEN beach for another chance of snorkeling or scuba diving to see the wreck ship of the USS Liberty ship that sunk during the world war II.And then continuing by visiting Pura Ponjok Batu founded in 16th century by Javanese Hindu priest Dang Hyang Nirartha, Air Saneh natural swimming pool, Pura Meduwe Karang for the Lord Possessing the ground, Pura Dalem Jagaraga an elaborate old temple of the dead, Pura Beji Sangsit a beautifully carved Subak temple.Check in for hotel at Lovina. Afternoon, visiting Banjar Hot spring and Buddhist Monastry. Overnight and dinner: Lovina.

    Day 4: Lovina/Bedugul

    Early morning going for a sunrise cruise in search for the dolphin. Breakfast and relaxing on the beach or another snorkeling. In the afternoon we are going to drive on to the central mountain through the scenic village of MUNDUK with its coffee and clove plantation. We are having a short exercise down to the mighty waterfall passing through the coffee and clove Forrest. A great chance for you to try the natural massage under the waterfall or even just for a short swim. Driving up through the scenic mountain village's lead us to a spot for bird eye view overlooking the two beautiful lakes of TAMBLINGAN AND BUYAN. A visit to the wonderful lake temple of ULUN DANU BERATAN a temple in the middle of the lake dedicated for the goddess of the prosperity or the lake, which is being worship by the local rice farmers. CANDIKUNING traditional fruit, vegetables, spices and flowers market. Overnight and dinner: BEDUGUL.

    Day 5: Bedugul/next Bali destination

    After breakfast a visit to the BALI BOTANICAL GARDEN will start our last day tour. Afterward a scenic drive down the hill will lead us to the village of JATILUWIH, which is known for its truly marvelous rice terrace view. BATUKARU temple an old sacred site in the middle of Bali rain forest. TANAH LOT a 16th century temple in the middle of the ocean for enjoying its wonderful sunset. Transfer to your next destination, END OF TRIP.

    Bali tours 7 Days

    DAY 01: UBUD - ARRIVAL

    On arrival at international Airport of Denpasar, meeting service and transfer to Ubud area for accommodation.

    DAY 02: UBUD

    Breakfast at hotel. Drive to Batubulan village to attend Barong and Keris dance performance. Then visit the art and handicraft villages of Celuk, Batuan, Mas and Ubud, to witness gold and silver work, hand weaving woodcarving and painting. Proceed to Goa Gajah (Elephant Cave), a Hindu meditation center dating back to the 11th Century, then Tampak Siring to see the holy Spring water temple Tirta Empul. Next on to view the magnificent panorama of the still active Mt. Batur volcano and Lake Batur. Lunch at local restaurant. After lunch drive down to see Besakih - the mother temple of Bali. Return to hotel.

    DAY 03: UBUD - MUNDUK Drive to Bali Bird Park for having breakfast & tea with the birds and visiting the park where you will find to your enjoyment and delight hundreds of the rarest and most beautiful birds from every corner of the earth and 250 exotic bird species from the multitude of Indonesia Island. Then exploring to the North to Mengwi - the royal family temple and the picturesque view of Bedugul. Stop can be made at local market to know how people selling and buying their daily life. On to Ulun Danu Temple - a serene temple located on the shores of Beratan Lake. Then drive to Munduk-the hinterland of Bali for accommodation at Puri Lumbung Cottages.

    DAY 04: MUNDUK - PEMUTERAN Breakfast at hotel. Walk around Munduk area, and then continue your roundtrip to Pemuteran - a village on the northern cost of the island via scenic road, cloves, and coffee plantation and rice terraces. Stop at Banjar - natural hot spring water as a public bathing area. Upon arrival in Pemuteran check in Taman Sari Hotel.

    DAY 05: PEMUTERAN - PUPUAN After having breakfast visiting West Bali National Park to see Bali Starling Bird (leucopsar rothschildi) a small white bird with brilliant blue streaks around its eyes and black tipped wings and other animals gracing the land are deer, civets, monkeys and Java Buffalo. Drive to Pupuan - an untouched village as Bali was 20 years ago where you can watch farmers working on their rice-fields using traditional system for hundreds of years. Accommodation at Cempaka Blimbing Bungalow.

    DAY 06: PUPUAN - KUTA Breakfast at hotel. Soft trekking and hiking around the village where you can watch how the famous trees grow such as Durian, Manggoestin, Cacao, Pineapples, Jackfruit, Nutmeg, Ginger, pepper and many others. Then drive to Alas Kedaton monkey forest; stop at Lukluk to see Subak irrigation sub-division system. Proceed to Tanah Lot Temple, which was built on a small, offshore island of barren-rock. Continue to Kuta Area for accommodation at your Hotel choice.

    DAY 07: KUTA - OUT Breakfast at hotel. Morning free at leisure until time to transfer to the Airport to depart to your next destination.
