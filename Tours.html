Background:

Best of DIBAKTI MULYATOURS was initially established in 2005 by a team of travel professionals led by Mr, Teguh who has more than 17 year of experience within the industry. Over the past two decades the company has developed rapidly and has emerged as one of Indonesia s most progressive inbound and outbound travel operators.

Annually Best of DIBAKTI MULYA Tours handles foreign incentive tour groups to Indonesia catering for up to 800 participants and we have become expert in pre-determining the needs and requirements for each successive group. We also rely on a strong network of hospitality professionals and their services including hotels and airlines who play a major role in assisting us to cater to incentive, meeting and social group travellers.

Over the years DIBAKTI MULYATours has expanded in order to meet growing market demands. This has included the strategic placement of our head office in the heart of Surabaya's business district and launching branches in other major cities for convenience. Our modern offices are equipped with the very latest in computer technology for efficient online access and a complete database of important information. We currently have a total of 2 branches throughout Indonesia located in Surabaya and Bali,
