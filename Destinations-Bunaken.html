Bunaken Tours

Manado fun Trips 6 Days

Destination Information:

Day 1 – Tangkoko National Park

The 6 Day Manado Tour begins with your arrival to the Sam Ratulangi Airport where you will meet the representative for the tour and be transferred directly to a local restaurant and then travel overland to the Tangkoko National Park where you will eat and spend the overnight stay at the Tangkoko cottage.

Day 2 - Jungle Exploration and Animal Tour

The second day of The 6 Day Manado Tour begins early in the morning when you will be able to trek down the coastline of Tangkoko looking for the Black Macaque monkeys that are the smallest monkeys in the world weighing under 200 grams and measuring only 15cm long. This is the best way to witness these incredible animals as they feed in this area very early in the morning and are visible in the trees and hollow areas where they hind and search for food. You will then return to the cottage for breakfast and continue the tour seeing other monkeys and the rare birds of this jungle area before returning to the Tangkoko cottage for your dinner and the overnight stay.

Day 3 – Traveling to Tomohon via Waruga and Minahasa

After eating breakfast you will drive through Waruga where you will see the ancient cemetery of Minahasa and the Japanese caves and the lake at Tondano. You will east at the floating restaurants on the lake and then visit the sulfuric lake of Linow located on a volcano where you can see the bubbling gases and the majestic scene of the are. You will then return to a resort at Tomohon

Day 4 – Bunaken Island Snorkeling

The fourth day of The Six-Day Manado Tour begins as usual with breakfast at your hotel and then you will have the opportunity to snorkel around the beautiful island of Bunaken. You will make this journey by sailing a traditional boat before switching to the glass bottom boat, which is used for the snorkeling trip and will take you to the classic sites around the Bunaken marine park. You will eat on this island with incredible views and then make the trip back to the cottage where you will spend the night.

Day 5 – Siladen Islands cruise and Snorkeling

After breakfast on the fifth day of The Six-Day Manado Tour you will head to the Siladen islands where you will continue snorkeling at Likuang and other spots that are renowned for amazing marine biodiversity and incredible beauty. After this equally as fascinating and tranquil experience you will then head back to the cottage at Bunaken.

Day 6 – Shopping and Airport Transfer

You will spend the beginning of your last day of The 6 Day Manado Tour by sailing to Manado where you will be able to shop in the Kawanua market and buy handicrafts and unusual items before making the transfer back to the airport for your next destination.
