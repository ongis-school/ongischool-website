Lake Toba Tour 4 Days

Day 1 : Medan – Brastagi

Brastagi Cottages or similar Arr in Medan Airport. Meet and greet at the airport then start on Medan city tour. Visit Maimoon Palace, the palace of Old Deli Kingdom and the Grand Mosque of Medan Also visit the temple of East Mountain, a Chinese temple with its unique characteristics. After that start on a two-hour drive to Berastagi, a highland resort with its refreshing weather and magnificent mountain scenery, through some part of the lush tropical rain forest of Sumatra. Reaching Berastagi, visit Peceran – a village with a number of traditional houses that are still being used. Then visit the fruit market of Brastagi. Check in hotel.

Day 2 : Brastagi – Parapat – Samosir Island

Toledo Inn or Sopo Toba Hotel Depart for Parapat – a famous lake resort lies on the east shore of Lake Toba. See the slender Sipiso-piso Waterfall drops into the northern tip shore of Lake Toba from hundred meter height. Visit the ancient King's Palace of Simalungun at Pematang Purba. Proceed to Parapat through the fertile vegetable and fruit farms, pine tree forest and spectacular view of Lake Toba along most part of the journey. Arrive in Parapat, leave for Samosir, a huge lovely island in the middle of Lake Toba, in 45-minute ferry ride. Check in hotel.

Day 3 : Samosir Tour – Medan

Tiara (4*) or Soechi (4*) or similar After breakfast, start on Samosir Tour by boat. Visit the Tomb of King Sidabutar and Batak Toba Village at Tomok. Then to Ambarita, a traditional village with ancient megalithic furniture in front of the traditional house. Afternoon return to Medan, the capital city of North Sumatra which is about 175 km away. En route, first hour pass by several small towns: Pematang Siantar and Tebing Tinggi, and then followed by vast view of rubber, oil palm and paddy terraces. Arrive in Medan, check in hotel.

Day 4 : Departure

Free at own activities until the time to be transferred to airport for departure.

    • Lake Toba – Bohorok 6 Days

    Day 1 : Medan – Parapat – Samosir Island

    Arrive at Medan Airport, after a short meeting service at the airport depart for Parapat – famous mountain and lake resort on the east shore of the immense volcanic Lake Toba, is about 175 km from Medan. En route, first hour pass by several small towns and then followed by vast view of rubber, oil palm, paddy terraces, etc. Arrive in Parapat, leave for Samosir, a huge lovely island in the middle of Lake Toba, in 45-minute ferry ride. Check in hotel.

    Day 2 : Samosir Island Tour

    After breakfast, start on Samosir Island Tour by boat. Visit the Tomb of King Sidabutar and Batak Toba Village at Tomok. Then to Ambarita, a traditional village with ancient megalithic furniture in front of the traditional house and also visit the Village of Simanindo to watch folkdance performance of Batak Toba people. Afternoon return to hotel.

    Day 3 : Samosir Island – Brastagi

    After breakfast depart for Brastagi via the Ring Road of Lake Toba. En route pass by pine tree plantation, the magnificent scenery of Lake Toba, fertile vegetable and fruit farms. Visit the ancient King's Palace of Simalungun at Pematang Purba. Proceed to the slender Sipiso-piso Waterfall which drops into Lake Toba from hundred of meter height. Arrive in Brastagi, a refreshing mountain resort dominated by two active volcanoes. Visit the fruit market and the traditional Batak Karo village – Peceran. Then check in hotel.

    Day 4 : Brastagi – Medan – Bohorok

    Depart for Bohorok via Medan in 5 hour drive. First, leave Brastagi for Medan via some part of the lush tropical rain forest of Sumatera in two-hour time. Arrive in Medan, after a short rest, continue journey to Bohorok via some small towns and drive through vast view of rubber, oil palm plantations, and some countryside landscape. The road is till bad in some places. Arrive in Bohorok, check in hotel.

    Day 5 : Bohorok - Medan

    Start trekking to see orang utan. First walk along the riverbank to the crossing point and cross the river by small canoe to the Orang Utan rehabilitation center. Hike up the hill through the rain forest to see the activities of those illegally captured and young orang utans which are trained to return to their natural habitat. Enter the station at 7 am to see orang utan feeding process. After the visit, walk back to your assembling point. Afternoon return to Medan, the capital city of North Sumatra province. Check in hotel for overnight.

    Day 6 : Trsf Out / Departure
