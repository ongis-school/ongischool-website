Toraja Adventures 4 Days / 3 Days

Day 01. Ujung Pandang to Rantepao.

Upon arrival at Hassanudin airport, meeting service and direct depart for Toraja. Approximately 8 hours trip takes across Bugis villages with their typical houses on Pole, along magnificent mountain passes. Lunch will be served at seafood restaurant in Pare a small port 155 km north of Ujung Pandang. Late afternoon arrive in Rantepao, Check-in hotel. Dinner and overnight.

Day 02. Rantepao

After breakfast hotel visiting the cliff tombs at Lemo, royal Tombs at Suaya, the Baby Grave on tree at Sanggala. After lunch continue to Marante and Manggal. Dinner in the Hotel and Overnight.

Day 03. Rantepao - Ujung Pandang

After breakfast, return to Ujung Pandang. Lunch will be served at seafood Restaurant in Pare Pare. Dinner and accommodation at Ujung Pandang.

Day 04. Ujung Pandang - Next Destination

After breakfast, free at leisure till time for transfer to the airport for flight to next destination

Included:

  • • 2 Nights in Toraja and 1 Night in Ujung Pandang
  • • All transfers, Tours as per itinerary, air-conditioned vehicle, full board, entrance and donation fees, Hotel accommodation.

    • Toraja Adventures 7 Days / 6 Days

    Day 01. Ujung Pandang - Bira

    Morning arrival in Ujung Pandang . After meeting service, a short city tour of Ujung Pandang visiting among other the 17th century dutch fortress (fort Rotterdam) and Bundt's Orchid Garden and coral collection. After lunch (at your own expenses) and depart for Bira. Simple dinner and accommodation in Bira.

    Day 02. Bira

    After breakfast, depart for Tanaberu to witness the traditional Bugis ways of boat building then proceed to Kajang to visit Konjo tribe in the isolated village and with the luck you might witness local weaving of sarongs. Return to Bira for dinner hotel and overnight.

    Day 03. Bira - Bone

    Morning free and opportunity to swim at the white sandy beach. After lunch depart for Bone, a capital of the influential Kingdom of Bone. Dinner hotel and overnight.

    Day 04. Bone - Toraja

    After simple breakfast depart to Toraja. Lunch en route. Dinner and accommodation in Toraja.

    Day 05. Toraja (Rantepao)

    After breakfast hotel visiting: The clift Tombs at Lemo, the Royal Tombs at Suaya/Sanggala, the village Ke'Tu'Su, after lunch at Chinese restaurant in Rantepao, continue to visit the family compound with tipical Toraja houses at Siguntu and the burial cave at Londa. Dinner hotel and overnight.

    Day 06. Toraja (Pa'lawa - To'Barana)

    After breakfast visit the village of Pa'lawa and To'barana/Sa'Dan. Lunch in Chinese restaurant in Rantepao

    Day 07. Rantepao - Ujungpandang

    After breakfast depart for Ujungpandang Hasanudin airport in time for the flight to your next destination. Dipending on the time for depature. Lunch will be served either in Chinese restaurant I Pare pare or airport restaurant.

    Included:

  • • 2 nights in Bira, 1 night in Bone, 3 Nights in Toraja
  • • All transfers, tour as per itinerary, air-conditioned vehicle, full board, entrance fees, hotel accommodation.

    Toraja Tours 3 Days

    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL - PAREPARE - TORAJA( L )

    Upon arrival at Makassar airport. Meeting service. Transfer to Toraja Land with 8 hours drive-trip takes across Bugis village with their typical houses built on poles along the magnificent mountainous ranges. Lunch will be served servedat Bukit Indah Restaurant in Pare-Pare, a small port situated 155 kms north of UjungPandang. Refreshment at the Top-Hill of Puncak Lakawan , the erotic mountain view.Late afternoon arrive in Toraja Land check-in at hotel for your accommodation and dinner

    DAY 02 : TORAJA TOUR ( B.L)

    Breakfast at hotel. Full day visiting : the stone grave with tau-tau installed on a high cliff balcony overlooking the green valley of the Toraja at LEMO,The royal family tombs at SUAYA. and then we continue on to royal family tombs at SUAYA. We shall then make a easy walk to see a giant-tree made as burial site for a new born-baby who died before teething at KAMBIRA. After Lunch, we drive to The traditional carving village at KETE KESU, Natural burial cave at LONDA. The typical village at NANGGALA with 16 rice-barns standing in a raw as a sign of their wealthy status inToraja community. Then continue on to visit the ancient house-grave at MARANTE. Late afternoon back to hotel for overnight and dinner.

    DAY 03 : TORAJA - PARE PARE - AIRPORT ( B.L )

    Breakfast at hotel. Leaving Toraja Land for UjungPandang. Lunch at KUPA Restaurant in Pare-Pare. After lunch, proceed to UjungPandang and directly to the Airport for your onward flight.
